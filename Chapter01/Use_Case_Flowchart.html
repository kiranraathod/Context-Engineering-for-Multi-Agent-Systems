Workflow Summary

1. Scope The process begins by cleaning the raw transcript. It isolates facts from noise (g2) and identifies what is strictly "new" information compared to previous history (g3).
2. Investigation Instead of just summarizing, the AI acts as an analyst. It looks for emotional subtext (g4) and actively proposes solutions to problems found in the text (g5).
3. Action Insights are useless without action. The final layer structures the data into a table (g6) and drafts a ready-to-send email (g7), completing the work.
